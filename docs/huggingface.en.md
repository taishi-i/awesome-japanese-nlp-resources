This page lists Japanese NLP-specific models and datasets available on Hugging Face. Currently, it includes 187 models and 165 datasets.

## Models
### text-generation
 * [NVIDIA-Nemotron-Nano-9B-v2-Japanese](https://huggingface.co/nvidia/NVIDIA-Nemotron-Nano-9B-v2-Japanese) - 📥 294k / ⭐ 138 / A 9‑billion‑parameter Japanese‑optimized LLM, NVIDIA Nemotron‑Nano‑9B‑v2‑Japanese, trained on data up to September 2024 with a hybrid Mamba‑2/MLP/4‑layer‑attention architecture, fine‑tuned on the Nemotron‑Personas‑Japan tool‑calling dataset, that optionally generates a controllable reasoning trace before producing a final answer and is available for commercial use.
 * [open-calm-3b](https://huggingface.co/cyberagent/open-calm-3b) - 📥 228k / ⭐ 21 / OpenCALM is a suite of decoder‑only Japanese transformer language models (160 M–6.8 B params) released by CyberAgent, Inc. under CC‑BY‑SA 4.0, trained on Japanese Wikipedia and Common Crawl, and usable via Hugging Face’s torch‑transformers.
 * [japanese-gpt-neox-small](https://huggingface.co/rinna/japanese-gpt-neox-small) - 📥 186k / ⭐ 15 / A 12‑layer, 768‑hidden Japanese GPT‑NeoX model trained on CC‑100, C4, and Wikipedia, compatible with Huggingface, with an optional toy prefix‑tuning weight that forces each sentence to end with a smiling‑face emoji.
 * [gpt-neox-japanese-2.7b](https://huggingface.co/abeja/gpt-neox-japanese-2.7b) - 📥 118k / ⭐ 59 / A 2.7‑B‑parameter Japanese GPT‑NeoX model trained on Japanese CC‑100 and OSCAR by ABEJA Inc, usable via Hugging Face Transformers pipelines or PyTorch and released under the MIT license.
 * [llm-jp-4-8b-thinking](https://huggingface.co/llm-jp/llm-jp-4-8b-thinking) - 📥 60k / ⭐ 39 / Provides the 8 B‑parameter LLM‑jp‑4‑8b‑thinking Japanese language model from NII, trained with pre‑/mid‑training and aligned via SFT/DPO, ready to use with torch‑transformers and detailed cookbook instructions.
 * [shisa-gamma-7b-v1](https://huggingface.co/augmxnt/shisa-gamma-7b-v1) - 📥 54k / ⭐ 18 / Fine‑tuned the Japanese Stable LM Base Gamma 7B with Shisa 7B data, achieving strong results on the JA MT‑Bench.
 * [llm-jp-3-150m](https://huggingface.co/llm-jp/llm-jp-3-150m) - 📥 42k / ⭐ 6 / LLM‑jp‑3‑150m—a 150‑M‑parameter Japanese language model from the National Institute of Informatics’ LLM R&D Center—is distributed in Hugging Face Transformers format, requiring torch ≥ 2.3.0, transformers ≥ 4.40.1, accelerate ≥ 0.29.3, flash‑attn ≥ 2.5.8, and was pretrained on Japanese Wikipedia, Common Crawl, WARP/PDF, WARP/HTML, and Kaken data using a unigram byte‑fallback tokenizer.
 * [llm-jp-4-8b-instruct](https://huggingface.co/llm-jp/llm-jp-4-8b-instruct) - 📥 26k / ⭐ 7 / llm‑jp‑4‑8b‑instruct is a 4.1 B‑parameter Japanese LLM from NII’s LLM‑jp‑4 series, pretrained on large corpora and then fine‑tuned solely with supervised instruction data (no DPO/REINFORCE) and accompanied by cookbook‑style usage guides and a byte‑fallback unigram tokenizer.
 * [karasu-1.1B](https://huggingface.co/lightblue/karasu-1.1B) - 📥 26k / ⭐ 7 / Pretrained TinyLlama in Japanese (≈50 k steps) built on ~3 B OSCAR/mC4 tokens, usable via HuggingFace Transformers or VLLM, created by Peter Devine, Sho Higuchi, Yuuki Yamanaka, Atom Sonoda, Shunichi Taniguchi, Tomioka Wataru, and Renju Aoki.
 * [Llama-3.1-Swallow-8B-Instruct-v0.3](https://huggingface.co/tokyotech-llm/Llama-3.1-Swallow-8B-Instruct-v0.3) - 📥 17k / ⭐ 24 / Llama 3.1 Swallow is a Japanese‑enhanced series of 8B/70B Llama 3.1 models, trained via continual pre‑training and Japanese‑specific instruction fine‑tuning, with the latest 8B‑Instruct‑v0.3 setting state‑of‑the‑art results on Japanese MT‑Bench.
 * [Llama-3-Swallow-8B-Instruct-v0.1](https://huggingface.co/tokyotech-llm/Llama-3-Swallow-8B-Instruct-v0.1) - 📥 12k / ⭐ 21 / Llama3 Swallow is a Japanese‑enhanced Meta Llama 3 family released July 1 2024, offering 8B and 70B variants in Instruct and chat forms fine‑tuned with SFT and Chat Vector on Megatron‑LM, and benchmarked on key Japanese NLP tasks.
 * [Llama-3-ELYZA-JP-8B](https://huggingface.co/elyza/Llama-3-ELYZA-JP-8B) - 📥 10k / ⭐ 148 / Llama‑3‑ELYZA‑JP‑8B is a Japanese‑enhanced, 8‑billion‑parameter Llama 3 model by ELYZA, fine‑tuned for Japanese on Meta‑Llama‑3‑8B‑Instruct.
 * [Mistral-Nemo-Japanese-Instruct-2408](https://huggingface.co/cyberagent/Mistral-Nemo-Japanese-Instruct-2408) - 📥 10k / ⭐ 48 / A Japanese continually‑pre‑trained Mistral‑Nemo model (Mistral‑Nemo‑Japanese‑Instruct‑2408) built on mistralai/Mistral‑Nemo‑Instruct‑2407, usable via transformers with device mapping and ChatML prompts, released under Apache‑2.0 by Ryosuke Ishigami.
 * [Llama-3-70B-japanese-suzume-vector-v0.1](https://huggingface.co/mmnga/Llama-3-70B-japanese-suzume-vector-v0.1) - 📥 8k / ⭐ 4 / Experimental Japanese model created by extracting differences between lightblue/suzume‑llama‑3‑8B‑japanese and Meta‑Llama‑3‑8B‑Instruct using a chat‑vector approach, upsampled and applied to Meta‑Llama‑3‑70B‑Instruct, showing little change and planning future scaling.
 * [japanese-stablelm-instruct-gamma-7B-GGUF](https://huggingface.co/TheBloke/japanese-stablelm-instruct-gamma-7B-GGUF) - 📥 7k / ⭐ 10 / Repository provides GGUF‑formatted, quantised model files for Stability AI’s Japanese StableLM Instruct Gamma 7B, created with Massed Compute hardware and part of TheBloke’s a16z‑funded LLM work.
 * [Gemma-2-Llama-Swallow-9b-pt-v0.1](https://huggingface.co/tokyotech-llm/Gemma-2-Llama-Swallow-9b-pt-v0.1) - 📥 7k / ⭐ 1 / Japanese‑enhanced, instruction‑tuned Gemma‑2 models built on Llama (2b/9b/27b pre‑train and instruction versions) released on May 19 2025, available via HuggingFace and the Swallow team’s website.
 * [japanese-gpt2-medium](https://huggingface.co/rinna/japanese-gpt2-medium) - 📥 6k / ⭐ 85 / Rinna’s 24‑layer, 1024‑hidden Japanese GPT‑2‑medium model, trained on CC‑100 and Wikipedia with SentencePiece tokenization, is available in the rinna/japanese‑pretrained‑models repo (MIT‑licensed, released April 7 2021, updated 25 Aug 2021).
 * [Swallow-13b-hf](https://huggingface.co/tokyotech-llm/Swallow-13b-hf) - 📥 5k / ⭐ 12 / Large language models by TokyoTech‑LLM, based on LLaMA‑2 and fine‑tuned with Japanese data (SFT), include Swallow‑7b/13b/70b variants and their instruct, NVE, and “plus” versions released from December 2023 to April 2024.
 * [TinySwallow-1.5B](https://huggingface.co/SakanaAI/TinySwallow-1.5B) - 📥 5k / ⭐ 35 / TinySwallow‑1.5B is a compact Japanese instruction‑following language model by Sakana AI and the Swallow Team that uses TAID distillation from Qwen2.5‑32B‑Instruct, is further pre‑trained on Japanese text, and is released under Apache 2.0 for research use only.
 * [sarashina2.2-3b-instruct-v0.1](https://huggingface.co/sbintuitions/sarashina2.2-3b-instruct-v0.1) - 📥 5k / ⭐ 38 / Offers an autoregressive Japanese language model (sarashina2.2‑3B‑instruct‑v0.1) from SB Intuitions, benchmarked against other models, with example usage scripts and a note that safety training is limited.
 * [japanese-gpt2-small](https://huggingface.co/rinna/japanese-gpt2-small) - 📥 5k / ⭐ 27 / rinna’s Japanese GPT‑2 small is a 12‑layer, 768‑hidden transformer trained on Japanese CC‑100 and Wikipedia, tokenized with SentencePiece, released under MIT on Aug 25 2021 (Hugging Face: rinna/japanese‑gpt2‑small, see https://arxiv.org/abs/2404.01657).
 * [Qwen3-Swallow-32B-RL-v0.2](https://huggingface.co/tokyotech-llm/Qwen3-Swallow-32B-RL-v0.2) - 📥 5k / ⭐ 1 / Qwen3‑Swallow v0.2 delivers 30‑B and 32‑B bilingual Japanese‑English LLMs trained with CPT, SFT, and RLVR that enhance Japanese accuracy, translation, math, and coding to match or surpass the original Qwen3, offering nine models (CPT, SFT, RL) and AWQ‑quantized versions, with GPT‑OSS‑Swallow also released.

 * [Swallow-7b-hf](https://huggingface.co/tokyotech-llm/Swallow-7b-hf) - 📥 4k / ⭐ 17 / The TokyoTech‑LLM repository provides the Swallow Llama‑2 family of LLaMA‑2 models, augmented with Japanese data, covering 7B, 13B, and 70B variants that include instruction‑tuned, NVE‑tuned, and a 7B Plus version released since December 2023.

 * [japanese-gpt-1b](https://huggingface.co/rinna/japanese-gpt-1b) - 📥 4k / ⭐ 108 / A 1.3‑B‑parameter, 24‑layer transformer GPT‑1B, trained on Japanese C4, CC‑100, and Wikipedia, was released by rinna Co. on January 26 2022 and is available under the MIT license.
 * [Qwen3-Swallow-8B-RL-v0.2](https://huggingface.co/tokyotech-llm/Qwen3-Swallow-8B-RL-v0.2) - 📥 3k / ⭐ 10 / Qwen3‑Swallow v0.2 delivers Japanese‑English LLMs (30B‑A3B and 32B) trained via CPT, SFT and RLVR, preserving strong math, coding, and reasoning abilities, with nine released models and AWQ‑quantized variants.
 * [llm-jp-3.1-1.8b-instruct4](https://huggingface.co/llm-jp/llm-jp-3.1-1.8b-instruct4) - 📥 3k / ⭐ 20 / Provides the 1.8 B‑parameter llm‑jp‑3.1‑1.8b‑instruct4 Japanese instruction‑tuned model from NII, compatible with Hugging Face Transformers and Torch ≥ 2.3.0, including pretrained and fine‑tuned checkpoints and usage examples.
 * [Llama-3.1-Swallow-8B-Instruct-v0.5](https://huggingface.co/tokyotech-llm/Llama-3.1-Swallow-8B-Instruct-v0.5) - 📥 3k / ⭐ 19 / Llama 3.1 Swallow is a set of 8‑B and 70‑B models that continue pre‑training Meta’s Llama 3.1 to boost Japanese language performance, then instruction‑fine‑tune on synthetic Japanese data—providing multiple released variants with improved conversational behavior comparable to gemma‑3‑27b‑it.
