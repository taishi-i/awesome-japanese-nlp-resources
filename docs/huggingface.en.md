## Models
### text-generation
 * [NVIDIA-Nemotron-Nano-9B-v2-Japanese](https://huggingface.co/nvidia/NVIDIA-Nemotron-Nano-9B-v2-Japanese) - 📥 352k / ⭐ 134 / A 9‑billion‑parameter Japanese‑optimized LLM, NVIDIA Nemotron‑Nano‑9B‑v2‑Japanese, trained on data up to September 2024 with a hybrid Mamba‑2/MLP/4‑layer‑attention architecture, fine‑tuned on the Nemotron‑Personas‑Japan tool‑calling dataset, that optionally generates a controllable reasoning trace before producing a final answer and is available for commercial use.
 * [open-calm-3b](https://huggingface.co/cyberagent/open-calm-3b) - 📥 217k / ⭐ 20 / OpenCALM is a suite of decoder‑only Japanese transformer language models (160 M–6.8 B params) released by CyberAgent, Inc. under CC‑BY‑SA 4.0, trained on Japanese Wikipedia and Common Crawl, and usable via Hugging Face’s torch‑transformers.
 * [japanese-gpt-neox-small](https://huggingface.co/rinna/japanese-gpt-neox-small) - 📥 118k / ⭐ 15 / A 12‑layer, 768‑hidden Japanese GPT‑NeoX model trained on CC‑100, C4, and Wikipedia, compatible with Huggingface, with an optional toy prefix‑tuning weight that forces each sentence to end with a smiling‑face emoji.
 * [gpt-neox-japanese-2.7b](https://huggingface.co/abeja/gpt-neox-japanese-2.7b) - 📥 92k / ⭐ 58 / A 2.7‑B‑parameter Japanese GPT‑NeoX model trained on Japanese CC‑100 and OSCAR by ABEJA Inc, usable via Hugging Face Transformers pipelines or PyTorch and released under the MIT license.
 * [llm-jp-4-8b-thinking](https://huggingface.co/llm-jp/llm-jp-4-8b-thinking) - 📥 35k / ⭐ 30 / Provides the 8 B‑parameter LLM‑jp‑4‑8b‑thinking Japanese language model from NII, trained with pre‑/mid‑training and aligned via SFT/DPO, ready to use with torch‑transformers and detailed cookbook instructions.
 * [LFM2.5-1.2B-JP-GGUF](https://huggingface.co/LiquidAI/LFM2.5-1.2B-JP-GGUF) - 📥 32k / ⭐ 29 / LFM2.5‑1.2B‑JP is a 1.2 B‑parameter Japanese text generation model built on the LFM2.5 hybrid architecture, optimized for generation and completion tasks, hosted on Hugging Face and runnable via llama.cpp.
 * [shisa-gamma-7b-v1](https://huggingface.co/augmxnt/shisa-gamma-7b-v1) - 📥 22k / ⭐ 18 / Fine‑tuned the Japanese Stable LM Base Gamma 7B with Shisa 7B data, achieving strong results on the JA MT‑Bench.
 * [llm-jp-3-150m](https://huggingface.co/llm-jp/llm-jp-3-150m) - 📥 22k / ⭐ 5 / LLM‑jp‑3‑150m—a 150‑M‑parameter Japanese language model from the National Institute of Informatics’ LLM R&D Center—is distributed in Hugging Face Transformers format, requiring torch ≥ 2.3.0, transformers ≥ 4.40.1, accelerate ≥ 0.29.3, flash‑attn ≥ 2.5.8, and was pretrained on Japanese Wikipedia, Common Crawl, WARP/PDF, WARP/HTML, and Kaken data using a unigram byte‑fallback tokenizer.
 * [llm-jp-3.1-1.8b-instruct4](https://huggingface.co/llm-jp/llm-jp-3.1-1.8b-instruct4) - 📥 14k / ⭐ 20 / Provides the 1.8 B‑parameter llm‑jp‑3.1‑1.8b‑instruct4 Japanese instruction‑tuned model from NII, compatible with Hugging Face Transformers and Torch ≥ 2.3.0, including pretrained and fine‑tuned checkpoints and usage examples.
 * [japanese-stablelm-instruct-gamma-7B-GGUF](https://huggingface.co/TheBloke/japanese-stablelm-instruct-gamma-7B-GGUF) - 📥 12k / ⭐ 10 / Repository provides GGUF‑formatted, quantised model files for Stability AI’s Japanese StableLM Instruct Gamma 7B, created with Massed Compute hardware and part of TheBloke’s a16z‑funded LLM work.
 * [Wanabi-Novelist-12B-i1-GGUF](https://huggingface.co/mradermacher/Wanabi-Novelist-12B-i1-GGUF) - 📥 10k / ⭐ 1 / Collection of GGUF quantizations (imatrix and static) for Wanabi‑Novelist‑12B, listing numerous precision levels and sizes, with usage guidance, download links, and supplementary resources.
 * [Llama-3-Swallow-8B-Instruct-v0.1](https://huggingface.co/tokyotech-llm/Llama-3-Swallow-8B-Instruct-v0.1) - 📥 9k / ⭐ 21 / Llama3 Swallow is a Japanese‑enhanced Meta Llama 3 family released July 1 2024, offering 8B and 70B variants in Instruct and chat forms fine‑tuned with SFT and Chat Vector on Megatron‑LM, and benchmarked on key Japanese NLP tasks.
 * [Llama-3-ELYZA-JP-8B](https://huggingface.co/elyza/Llama-3-ELYZA-JP-8B) - 📥 9k / ⭐ 147 / Llama‑3‑ELYZA‑JP‑8B is a Japanese‑enhanced, 8‑billion‑parameter Llama 3 model by ELYZA, fine‑tuned for Japanese on Meta‑Llama‑3‑8B‑Instruct.
 * [llm-jp-3-1.8b-instruct](https://huggingface.co/llm-jp/llm-jp-3-1.8b-instruct) - 📥 8k / ⭐ 25 / Hugging Face‑compatible Japanese‑centric transformer models (llm‑jp‑3‑1.8b, 1.8b‑instruct, 3.7b, 3.7b‑instruct, 13b, 13b‑instruct, 17.2b‑beta1, 17.2b‑beta1‑instruct) from the National Institute of Informatics, pre‑trained on diverse Japanese and English corpora (including Wikipedia, Common Crawl, WARP, Kaken, Dolma) and requiring torch ≥ 2.3, transformers ≥ 4.40, accelerate, and flash‑attn.
 * [llm-jp-4-32b-a3b-thinking](https://huggingface.co/llm-jp/llm-jp-4-32b-a3b-thinking) - 📥 8k / ⭐ 22 / A 32‑billion‑parameter Japanese transformer LLM (llm‑jp‑4‑32b‑a3b‑thinking) from the National Institute of Informatics, pre‑trained and aligned via supervised fine‑tuning and direct preference optimization—no reinforcement learning—using a unigram byte‑fallback tokenizer.
 * [Llama-3-70B-japanese-suzume-vector-v0.1](https://huggingface.co/mmnga/Llama-3-70B-japanese-suzume-vector-v0.1) - 📥 8k / ⭐ 4 / Experimental Japanese model created by extracting differences between lightblue/suzume‑llama‑3‑8B‑japanese and Meta‑Llama‑3‑8B‑Instruct using a chat‑vector approach, upsampled and applied to Meta‑Llama‑3‑70B‑Instruct, showing little change and planning future scaling.
 * [shisa-v2.1-qwen3-8b](https://huggingface.co/shisa-ai/shisa-v2.1-qwen3-8b) - 📥 8k / ⭐ 8 / Shisa V2.1 introduces upgraded bilingual Japanese‑English chat models—built from a refreshed dataset with enhanced instruction, translation and politeness fine‑tuning—across variants like Llama 3.2, Qwen 3‑8B and UnPhi‑4 14B, delivering noticeable performance gains.

 * [karasu-1.1B](https://huggingface.co/lightblue/karasu-1.1B) - 📥 5k / ⭐ 7 / Pretrained TinyLlama in Japanese (≈50 k steps) built on ~3 B OSCAR/mC4 tokens, usable via HuggingFace Transformers or VLLM, created by Peter Devine, Sho Higuchi, Yuuki Yamanaka, Atom Sonoda, Shunichi Taniguchi, Tomioka Wataru, and Renju Aoki.

 * [japanese-gpt2-small](https://huggingface.co/rinna/japanese-gpt2-small) - 📥 5k / ⭐ 26 / rinna’s Japanese GPT‑2 small is a 12‑layer, 768‑hidden transformer trained on Japanese CC‑100 and Wikipedia, tokenized with SentencePiece, released under MIT on Aug 25 2021 (Hugging Face: rinna/japanese‑gpt2‑small, see https://arxiv.org/abs/2404.01657).
