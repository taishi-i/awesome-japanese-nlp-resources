## Models
### text-generation
 * [NVIDIA-Nemotron-Nano-9B-v2-Japanese](https://huggingface.co/nvidia/NVIDIA-Nemotron-Nano-9B-v2-Japanese) - 📥 537k / ⭐ 131 / A 9‑billion‑parameter Japanese‑optimized LLM, NVIDIA Nemotron‑Nano‑9B‑v2‑Japanese, trained on data up to September 2024 with a hybrid Mamba‑2/MLP/4‑layer‑attention architecture, fine‑tuned on the Nemotron‑Personas‑Japan tool‑calling dataset, that optionally generates a controllable reasoning trace before producing a final answer and is available for commercial use.
 * [japanese-gpt-neox-small](https://huggingface.co/rinna/japanese-gpt-neox-small) - 📥 237k / ⭐ 15 / A 12‑layer, 768‑hidden Japanese GPT‑NeoX model trained on CC‑100, C4, and Wikipedia, compatible with Huggingface, with an optional toy prefix‑tuning weight that forces each sentence to end with a smiling‑face emoji.
 * [open-calm-3b](https://huggingface.co/cyberagent/open-calm-3b) - 📥 212k / ⭐ 20 / OpenCALM is a suite of decoder‑only Japanese transformer language models (160 M–6.8 B params) released by CyberAgent, Inc. under CC‑BY‑SA 4.0, trained on Japanese Wikipedia and Common Crawl, and usable via Hugging Face’s torch‑transformers.
 * [gpt-neox-japanese-2.7b](https://huggingface.co/abeja/gpt-neox-japanese-2.7b) - 📥 116k / ⭐ 58 / A 2.7‑B‑parameter Japanese GPT‑NeoX model trained on Japanese CC‑100 and OSCAR by ABEJA Inc, usable via Hugging Face Transformers pipelines or PyTorch and released under the MIT license.
 * [LFM2.5-1.2B-JP-GGUF](https://huggingface.co/LiquidAI/LFM2.5-1.2B-JP-GGUF) - 📥 35k / ⭐ 29 / LFM2.5‑1.2B‑JP is a 1.2 B‑parameter Japanese text generation model built on the LFM2.5 hybrid architecture, optimized for generation and completion tasks, hosted on Hugging Face and runnable via llama.cpp.
 * [llm-jp-3.1-13b-instruct4](https://huggingface.co/llm-jp/llm-jp-3.1-13b-instruct4) - 📥 33k / ⭐ 19 / LLM‑jp‑3.1‑13b‑instruct4 is a 13‑B, instruction‑pre‑trained Japanese language model developed by NII’s R&D Center, released as a Hugging‑Face Transformers checkpoint with a UNIGRAM‑byte‑fallback tokenizer.
 * [llm-jp-3.1-13b](https://huggingface.co/llm-jp/llm-jp-3.1-13b) - 📥 24k / ⭐ 2 / llm‑jp‑3.1‑13b is a 13 B‑parameter transformer LLM from NII’s R&D Center, pre‑trained on Japanese text and available in Hugging Face format with torch ≥2.3, transformers ≥4.40, plus fine‑tuned checkpoints and a unigram byte‑fallback tokenizer.
 * [shisa-gamma-7b-v1](https://huggingface.co/augmxnt/shisa-gamma-7b-v1) - 📥 21k / ⭐ 18 / Fine‑tuned the Japanese Stable LM Base Gamma 7B with Shisa 7B data, achieving strong results on the JA MT‑Bench.
 * [Llama-3-ELYZA-JP-8B](https://huggingface.co/elyza/Llama-3-ELYZA-JP-8B) - 📥 16k / ⭐ 146 / Llama‑3‑ELYZA‑JP‑8B is a Japanese‑enhanced, 8‑billion‑parameter Llama 3 model by ELYZA, fine‑tuned for Japanese on Meta‑Llama‑3‑8B‑Instruct.
 * [llm-jp-3.1-1.8b-instruct4](https://huggingface.co/llm-jp/llm-jp-3.1-1.8b-instruct4) - 📥 13k / ⭐ 20 / Provides the 1.8 B‑parameter llm‑jp‑3.1‑1.8b‑instruct4 Japanese instruction‑tuned model from NII, compatible with Hugging Face Transformers and Torch ≥ 2.3.0, including pretrained and fine‑tuned checkpoints and usage examples.

 * [Llama-3-Swallow-8B-Instruct-v0.1](https://huggingface.co/tokyotech-llm/Llama-3-Swallow-8B-Instruct-v0.1) - 📥 10k / ⭐ 21 / Llama3 Swallow is a Japanese‑enhanced Meta Llama 3 family released July 1 2024, offering 8B and 70B variants in Instruct and chat forms fine‑tuned with SFT and Chat Vector on Megatron‑LM, and benchmarked on key Japanese NLP tasks.
 * [llm-jp-3-1.8b-instruct](https://huggingface.co/llm-jp/llm-jp-3-1.8b-instruct) - 📥 8k / ⭐ 25 / Hugging Face‑compatible Japanese‑centric transformer models (llm‑jp‑3‑1.8b, 1.8b‑instruct, 3.7b, 3.7b‑instruct, 13b, 13b‑instruct, 17.2b‑beta1, 17.2b‑beta1‑instruct) from the National Institute of Informatics, pre‑trained on diverse Japanese and English corpora (including Wikipedia, Common Crawl, WARP, Kaken, Dolma) and requiring torch ≥ 2.3, transformers ≥ 4.40, accelerate, and flash‑attn.
 * [llm-jp-3-150m](https://huggingface.co/llm-jp/llm-jp-3-150m) - 📥 8k / ⭐ 5 / LLM‑jp‑3‑150m—a 150‑M‑parameter Japanese language model from the National Institute of Informatics’ LLM R&D Center—is distributed in Hugging Face Transformers format, requiring torch ≥ 2.3.0, transformers ≥ 4.40.1, accelerate ≥ 0.29.3, flash‑attn ≥ 2.5.8, and was pretrained on Japanese Wikipedia, Common Crawl, WARP/PDF, WARP/HTML, and Kaken data using a unigram byte‑fallback tokenizer.

 * [Llama-3.1-Swallow-8B-Instruct-v0.5](https://huggingface.co/tokyotech-llm/Llama-3.1-Swallow-8B-Instruct-v0.5) - 📥 8k / ⭐ 18 / Llama 3.1 Swallow is a set of 8‑B and 70‑B models that continue pre‑training Meta’s Llama 3.1 to boost Japanese language performance, then instruction‑fine‑tune on synthetic Japanese data—providing multiple released variants with improved conversational behavior comparable to gemma‑3‑27b‑it.

 * [Qwen3.5-35B-A3B-heretic-v2-ja-imatrix-GGUF](https://huggingface.co/k0ndra/Qwen3.5-35B-A3B-heretic-v2-ja-imatrix-GGUF) - 📥 6k / ⭐ 2 / Japanese‑calibrated importance‑matrix GGUF quantizations (IQ3/IQ4) of the Qwen3.5‑35B‑A3B‑heretic‑v2 model, aimed at preserving Japanese generation quality while significantly dropping safety filters, are released under a user‑responsibility disclaimer.
