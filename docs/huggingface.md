 * [japanese-gpt-neox-small](https://huggingface.co/rinna/japanese-gpt-neox-small) - 📥 275k / ⭐ 15 / A 12‑layer, 768‑hidden Japanese GPT‑NeoX model trained on CC‑100, C4, and Wikipedia, compatible with Huggingface, with an optional toy prefix‑tuning weight that forces each sentence to end with a smiling‑face emoji.
 * [open-calm-3b](https://huggingface.co/cyberagent/open-calm-3b) - 📥 151k / ⭐ 20 / OpenCALM is a suite of decoder‑only Japanese transformer language models (160 M–6.8 B params) released by CyberAgent, Inc. under CC‑BY‑SA 4.0, trained on Japanese Wikipedia and Common Crawl, and usable via Hugging Face’s torch‑transformers.
 * [gpt-neox-japanese-2.7b](https://huggingface.co/abeja/gpt-neox-japanese-2.7b) - 📥 91k / ⭐ 58 / A 2.7‑B‑parameter Japanese GPT‑NeoX model trained on Japanese CC‑100 and OSCAR by ABEJA Inc, usable via Hugging Face Transformers pipelines or PyTorch and released under the MIT license.
