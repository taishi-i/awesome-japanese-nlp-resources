## Models
### text-generation
 * [japanese-gpt-neox-small](https://huggingface.co/rinna/japanese-gpt-neox-small) - 📥 480k / ⭐ 15 / A 12‑layer, 768‑hidden Japanese GPT‑NeoX model trained on CC‑100, C4, and Wikipedia, compatible with Huggingface, with an optional toy prefix‑tuning weight that forces each sentence to end with a smiling‑face emoji.
 * [NVIDIA-Nemotron-Nano-9B-v2-Japanese](https://huggingface.co/nvidia/NVIDIA-Nemotron-Nano-9B-v2-Japanese) - 📥 444k / ⭐ 131 / A 9‑billion‑parameter Japanese‑optimized LLM, NVIDIA Nemotron‑Nano‑9B‑v2‑Japanese, trained on data up to September 2024 with a hybrid Mamba‑2/MLP/4‑layer‑attention architecture, fine‑tuned on the Nemotron‑Personas‑Japan tool‑calling dataset, that optionally generates a controllable reasoning trace before producing a final answer and is available for commercial use.
 * [open-calm-3b](https://huggingface.co/cyberagent/open-calm-3b) - 📥 219k / ⭐ 20 / OpenCALM is a suite of decoder‑only Japanese transformer language models (160 M–6.8 B params) released by CyberAgent, Inc. under CC‑BY‑SA 4.0, trained on Japanese Wikipedia and Common Crawl, and usable via Hugging Face’s torch‑transformers.
 * [gpt-neox-japanese-2.7b](https://huggingface.co/abeja/gpt-neox-japanese-2.7b) - 📥 118k / ⭐ 58 / A 2.7‑B‑parameter Japanese GPT‑NeoX model trained on Japanese CC‑100 and OSCAR by ABEJA Inc, usable via Hugging Face Transformers pipelines or PyTorch and released under the MIT license.
 * [llm-jp-3.1-13b-instruct4](https://huggingface.co/llm-jp/llm-jp-3.1-13b-instruct4) - 📥 40k / ⭐ 19 / LLM‑jp‑3.1‑13b‑instruct4 is a 13‑B, instruction‑pre‑trained Japanese language model developed by NII’s R&D Center, released as a Hugging‑Face Transformers checkpoint with a UNIGRAM‑byte‑fallback tokenizer.
 * [LFM2.5-1.2B-JP-GGUF](https://huggingface.co/LiquidAI/LFM2.5-1.2B-JP-GGUF) - 📥 39k / ⭐ 28 / LFM2.5‑1.2B‑JP is a 1.2 B‑parameter Japanese text generation model built on the LFM2.5 hybrid architecture, optimized for generation and completion tasks, hosted on Hugging Face and runnable via llama.cpp.
 * [llm-jp-3.1-13b](https://huggingface.co/llm-jp/llm-jp-3.1-13b) - 📥 24k / ⭐ 2 / llm‑jp‑3.1‑13b is a 13 B‑parameter transformer LLM from NII’s R&D Center, pre‑trained on Japanese text and available in Hugging Face format with torch ≥2.3, transformers ≥4.40, plus fine‑tuned checkpoints and a unigram byte‑fallback tokenizer.
 * [shisa-gamma-7b-v1](https://huggingface.co/augmxnt/shisa-gamma-7b-v1) - 📥 17k / ⭐ 18 / Fine‑tuned the Japanese Stable LM Base Gamma 7B with Shisa 7B data, achieving strong results on the JA MT‑Bench.
 * [Llama-3-ELYZA-JP-8B](https://huggingface.co/elyza/Llama-3-ELYZA-JP-8B) - 📥 14k / ⭐ 146 / Llama‑3‑ELYZA‑JP‑8B is a Japanese‑enhanced, 8‑billion‑parameter Llama 3 model by ELYZA, fine‑tuned for Japanese on Meta‑Llama‑3‑8B‑Instruct.
 * [TinySwallow-1.5B-Instruct](https://huggingface.co/SakanaAI/TinySwallow-1.5B-Instruct) - 📥 13k / ⭐ 56 / TinySwallow‑1.5B‑Instruct is a 1.5 B Japanese instruction‑tuned autoregressive language model distilled with TAID from Qwen2.5‑32B‑Instruct, intended for research use only.
 * [Llama-3-Swallow-8B-Instruct-v0.1](https://huggingface.co/tokyotech-llm/Llama-3-Swallow-8B-Instruct-v0.1) - 📥 11k / ⭐ 21 / Llama3 Swallow is a Japanese‑enhanced Meta Llama 3 family released July 1 2024, offering 8B and 70B variants in Instruct and chat forms fine‑tuned with SFT and Chat Vector on Megatron‑LM, and benchmarked on key Japanese NLP tasks.
 * [Llama-3.1-Swallow-8B-Instruct-v0.5](https://huggingface.co/tokyotech-llm/Llama-3.1-Swallow-8B-Instruct-v0.5) - 📥 11k / ⭐ 18 / Llama 3.1 Swallow is a set of 8‑B and 70‑B models that continue pre‑training Meta’s Llama 3.1 to boost Japanese language performance, then instruction‑fine‑tune on synthetic Japanese data—providing multiple released variants with improved conversational behavior comparable to gemma‑3‑27b‑it.
 * [japanese-gpt2-small](https://huggingface.co/rinna/japanese-gpt2-small) - 📥 10k / ⭐ 25 / rinna’s Japanese GPT‑2 small is a 12‑layer, 768‑hidden transformer trained on Japanese CC‑100 and Wikipedia, tokenized with SentencePiece, released under MIT on Aug 25 2021 (Hugging Face: rinna/japanese‑gpt2‑small, see https://arxiv.org/abs/2404.01657).
 * [NVIDIA-Nemotron-Nano-9B-v2-Japanese-gguf](https://huggingface.co/mmnga-o/NVIDIA-Nemotron-Nano-9B-v2-Japanese-gguf) - 📥 10k / ⭐ 50 / GGUF‑formatted NVIDIA‑Nemotron‑Nano‑9B‑v2‑Japanese, rebuilt from the imatrix dataset, and ready to run with llama.cpp on CUDA.
 * [Llama-3.1-Swallow-8B-Instruct-v0.3](https://huggingface.co/tokyotech-llm/Llama-3.1-Swallow-8B-Instruct-v0.3) - 📥 9k / ⭐ 24 / Llama 3.1 Swallow is a Japanese‑enhanced series of 8B/70B Llama 3.1 models, trained via continual pre‑training and Japanese‑specific instruction fine‑tuning, with the latest 8B‑Instruct‑v0.3 setting state‑of‑the‑art results on Japanese MT‑Bench.
 * [Llama-3-70B-japanese-suzume-vector-v0.1](https://huggingface.co/mmnga/Llama-3-70B-japanese-suzume-vector-v0.1) - 📥 9k / ⭐ 4 / Experimental Japanese model created by extracting differences between lightblue/suzume‑llama‑3‑8B‑japanese and Meta‑Llama‑3‑8B‑Instruct using a chat‑vector approach, upsampled and applied to Meta‑Llama‑3‑70B‑Instruct, showing little change and planning future scaling.
 * [GPT-OSS-Swallow-20B-RL-v0.1](https://huggingface.co/tokyotech-llm/GPT-OSS-Swallow-20B-RL-v0.1) - 📥 7k / ⭐ 18 / GPT‑OSS‑Swallow v0.1 delivers 20B and 120B bilingual Japanese‑English LLMs, trained via CPT, SFT, and RLVR, that match or surpass GPT‑OSS on math and coding tasks, released Feb 2026 with four SFT/RL variants and forthcoming quantized versions.
 * [llm-jp-3.1-1.8b-instruct4](https://huggingface.co/llm-jp/llm-jp-3.1-1.8b-instruct4) - 📥 7k / ⭐ 19 / Provides the 1.8 B‑parameter llm‑jp‑3.1‑1.8b‑instruct4 Japanese instruction‑tuned model from NII, compatible with Hugging Face Transformers and Torch ≥ 2.3.0, including pretrained and fine‑tuned checkpoints and usage examples.
 * [Qwen3-Swallow-8B-RL-v0.2](https://huggingface.co/tokyotech-llm/Qwen3-Swallow-8B-RL-v0.2) - 📥 5k / ⭐ 8 / Qwen3‑Swallow v0.2 delivers Japanese‑English LLMs (30B‑A3B and 32B) trained via CPT, SFT and RLVR, preserving strong math, coding, and reasoning abilities, with nine released models and AWQ‑quantized variants.
 * [Qwen3-Swallow-8B-CPT-v0.2](https://huggingface.co/tokyotech-llm/Qwen3-Swallow-8B-CPT-v0.2) - 📥 4k / ⭐ 1 / Bilingual 30 B‑ and 32 B‑parameter LLMs, Qwen3‑Swallow v0.2, are built with CPT, SFT, and RLVR to excel in Japanese, Japanese‑English translation, math, and coding, matching or surpassing Qwen3, and released in AWQ‑quantized form on Hugging Face.

 * [japanese-gpt-1b](https://huggingface.co/rinna/japanese-gpt-1b) - 📥 3k / ⭐ 107 / A 1.3‑B‑parameter, 24‑layer transformer GPT‑1B, trained on Japanese C4, CC‑100, and Wikipedia, was released by rinna Co. on January 26 2022 and is available under the MIT license.
